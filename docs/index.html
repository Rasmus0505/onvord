FEATURES

Built for the AI Native Era

Transforming how SOPs are created and presented, making them readable for both humans and AI.

Real-time Streaming STT

Powered by Deepgram Nova-2. Supports multi-language auto-detection. Narrate while you operate; your voice is automatically linked to the corresponding actions without post-editing.

Smart Action Capture

Automatically records clicks, inputs, and scrolls. Precisely distinguishes between 'text selection' and 'clicks', intelligently filtering out meaningless redundant actions.

Annotated Screenshots

Auto-captures the screen on every action, marking the exact click position with a high-contrast ring, ensuring clarity on both light and dark backgrounds.

Standalone Structured Export

One-click export to a self-contained HTML file. Send it to LLMs (like Claude or GPT-4V), and the AI instantly understands your intent and the exact CSS Node path.
